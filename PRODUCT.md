# Product Definition

## Register
brand

## Core Purpose
A personal portfolio website for a TCA70 (TCA70-1) student from Prince Royal's College. The website is a landing page to showcase the student's profile, achievements, and readiness for university admissions.

## Target Audience
University admission committees, professors, and interviewers evaluating candidates for TCA70-1 (Portfolio round) at top Thai universities (Chulalongkorn, Chiang Mai University, Mahidol, Ladkrabang).

## Vibe & Tone
- Professional yet youthful and vibrant.
- Ambitious, organized, and confident.
- Outstanding and memorable.

## Thematic Feature
The portfolio features a "University Theme Toggler" that adapts the brand colors to match the target university the interviewer is from, showing attention to detail and dedication.

## Anti-References
- Overly cluttered, complex dashboards (this is a brand/landing page, not a SaaS app).
- Generic "Resume/CV" templates with boring white/grey backgrounds.
- Unstructured layouts.
